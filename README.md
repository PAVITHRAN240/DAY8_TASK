ONLINE QUIZ APP
          A simple console-based quiz application built in Java.  
          It displays multiple-choice questions, accepts user input, and shows the final score with feedback.

SAMPLE OUTPUT:
Welcome to the Online Quiz App 
Enter the correct option letter (A/B/C/D)

1. What is the capital of France?
A. Berlin
B. Paris
C. Madrid
D. Rome
Your answer: B
Correct!
